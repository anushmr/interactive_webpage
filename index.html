Coffee

Ancestors of today's Oromo people in a region of Oromia in Ethiopia were the first to cultivate coffee plant and recognise the energising effect of the coffee, says Wikipedia. It is believed that coffee was first mentioned in 15th century in the Sufi monasteries in Yemen. It then spread in the Middle East, South India, Persia, other African countries, and then reached Europe and Asia.
